SOMETHING MAGNIFICENT IS BEING CRAFTED

The Legacy

Luxury is not defined by a price tag, but by the story behind the craft. For over 30 years, our family manufacturing unit in Mumbai has been the silent force behind India's most prestigious jewelry houses.

"We don't simply manufacture jewelry; we preserve history."

Today, we step out of the shadows. Rudra eliminates the middlemen to bring you showroom-quality Jadau and Kundan heritage designs directly from our workshop. Authentic, Honest, and Affordable.

MEET THE VISIONARY

Radhika Daga

CEO & Founder

Growing up amidst the clinking of hammers and the shimmer of gold dust in her father's factory in Mumbai, Radhika realized that the modern Indian woman wanted versatility. She wanted the grandeur of a Polki set, but the freedom to travel without fear.

Radhika founded Rudra with a singular mission: To democratize luxury. To give every woman the power to own a royal treasury with skin-safe, premium quality craftsmanship.

Tradition Meets Innovation

Where 300-year-old art meets AI precision.

AI-Calibrated Symmetry

We use advanced AI algorithms to analyze our Kundan settings. This ensures every stone is perfectly balanced, achieving geometric perfection that the human eye loves, while keeping the soul of handmade art.

Nano-Tech Plating

Our jewelry goes beyond standard polish. We use Nano-Technology based coating that forms an invisible shield, protecting the gold sheen from sweat and perfume for 3x longer durability.

The Art of Creation

From sketch to masterpiece.

1. The Blueprint
Every piece starts as a hand-drawn sketch by our master artisans, inspired by Mughal archives.
2. The Molding
Using high-grade brass alloys, the base structure is cast to perfection for durability.
3. The Finish
Stones are set by hand using traditional 'Jadau' techniques, finished with a premium gold polish that is skin-friendly.
